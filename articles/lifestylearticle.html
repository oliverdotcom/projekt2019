How to live climate friendly

No one likes to be lectured. But we all have to start doing our part, in the battle against climate change. Here are 3 things that you can start doing today.

BY OLIVER JÃœTZLER
11. OCTOBER 2019

1. Turn off the lights

Yes, your mother has probably yelled at you a million times for forgetting this, and now that you have finally moved out, you can rebel against it. But even though your mom probably was mostly concerned about the electrical bill, turning off the lights and other electronics is an easy way of helping the environment. And saving some cash.


2. Buy less

Producing new stuff like clothes and furniture is very bad for the environment. Take a trip to your local charity shop, and start digging. You might just like what you find. Also, try fixing you old stuff, instead of throwing it out.


3. Fly less

Travelling by airplane is convenient, fast and cheap. It's also bad for the environment. If you live in Europe, you have lots of oppurtunities to travel by train and bus. This is much better for the environment, and you discover things that you wouldn't have seen from above the skies.
